About the school

Founded by BENJELLOUN Ladies and SUIRE-SINACEUR in 1983 with 40 students, the School Al Jabr has grown year after year, reaching cruising speed in 1998-1999 with 1,000 enrolled.The aim of the establishment is not only to prepare students for the French Baccalaureate Diploma, but allow them access to a wide range of higher education, whether in Morocco or abroad, and in various languages (Arabic, french, English, Spanish and German).
